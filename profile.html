About Michal

We are busy, hard working moms who also happen to be premier trained and highly experienced Registered Dietitians in the city that never sleeps. Having spent nearly 15 years privately counseling individuals and families to meet their nutrition and wellness goals, we began thinking of all the ways we could provide the same level of customized care in a more accessible and affordable way, without that big apple price tag.

We know that nutrition is the best form of preventative medicine. You want the facts, but finding them, understanding them, and using them on a daily basis is just not effective without expert guidance.
